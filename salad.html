Salad Recipes for IBS Patients

Gentle Greens Salad
  • 2 cups mixed greens (such as lettuce, spinach, and arugula)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken breast, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Toss gently. Serve immediately.

Quinoa and Roasted Vegetable Salad
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 2 cups mixed roasted vegetables (such as broccoli, carrots, and sweet potatoes)
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Toss gently. Serve immediately.

Grilled Chicken and Avocado Salad
  • 1 cup grilled chicken breast, diced
  • 1 ripe avocado, diced
  • 2 cups mixed greens (such as lettuce, spinach, and arugula)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Toss gently. Serve immediately.

Lentil and Vegetable Salad
  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as carrots, zucchini, and bell peppers)
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Toss gently. Serve immediately.
