Smoothie Recipes for IBS Patients

Berry Banana Smoothie

A delicious and soothing smoothie.

  • 1/2 cup strawberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup blueberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 ripe banana
  • 1 cup lactose-free milk or almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup (optional)

Add all ingredients to a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.

Green Gut Soother Smoothie

A refreshing and calming smoothie.

  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/2 cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 kiwi, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 ripe banana
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • Juice of 1/2 lime

Add all ingredients to a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.

Tropical Delight Smoothie

A fruity and uplifting smoothie.

  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned in juice)
  • 1/2 cup mango chunks (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 ripe banana
  • 1 cup lactose-free yogurt or coconut yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water or coconut water

Add all ingredients to a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.

Avocado and Blueberry Smoothie

A creamy and nourishing smoothie.

  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 cup blueberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 ripe banana
  • 1 cup lactose-free milk or almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

Add all ingredients to a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.
